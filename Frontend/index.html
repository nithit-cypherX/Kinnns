Our Story

Kinnns was born from a passion for exquisite dining and cultural diversity. Located in the heart of Siam, Bangkok, we offer curated fine-dining courses inspired by flavors from around the world.
With each dish crafted by expert chefs, paired with live music and a refined atmosphere, we promise more than just a meal, an experience that indulges every sense.

Signature Dishes

Discover the essence of Kinnns through our signature creations. Enjoy the Truffle Mushroom & Prosciutto Pizza, a thin-crust base topped with wild mushrooms, mozzarella, prosciutto, arugula, and truffle oil. Savor the Ramen Carbonara, a rich fusion of creamy pasta and ramen with poached lobster, onsen egg, nori, and crispy pancetta. Finish with the Matcha Lava Cake , a warm matcha dessert with a gooey center, served with vanilla bean ice cream and black sesame crumble.
